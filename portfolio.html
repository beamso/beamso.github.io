General Assembly User Experience Design immersive final project

A redesign of an email marketing template and associated webpages for 10collective.

10collective perceived that their email marketing template needed improved information hierarchy, newer fonts, better responsive design for viewing the email on mobile devices, more areas for content to be added and new types of ads.

Screenshot of the original email - top.

  • Note the large space between the top of the email and the first Contractor ad.
  • 'Available Contractors' links to a page with all available contractors.
  • 'see more' links to an individual page for each contractor.
  • Call to action links are grey.

Screenshot of the original email - bottom.

  • 'see all contractors' links to the same page that 'Available Contractors' does.
  • Contractor ads are followed by links to blog content, followed by the email footer.

Screenshot of the original contractors page - top.

  • Note the large space between the top of the page and the available contractors.
  • 'Positions' link directs the user away from this page. Users did not expect this.
  • Calls to action are pink here.
  • 'candidates' and 'clients' in navigation could be confusing for some users.

Screenshot of the original contractors page - bottom.

  • Some users felt that the links underneath the small ads were calls to action. They are the tags for the category of the contractor.
  • The link to see the detail for the individual contractor is the title.
  • No search functionality is available.

Screenshot of the original contractor detail page.

  • Key skills are interspersed with content.
  • Note the repetition of role and location information.
  • Calls to action are black.
  • 'Enquire now' link is a 'mailto:' link, which may not work depending on the user's device, operating system or chosen mail client.

Sketch of new email.

Sketch of new contractors page.

Wireframe of Email from Axure.

  • Short distance between start of email and relevant content.
  • Contractors are grouped by category.
  • Information about contractors is structured.
  • More information about contractors is available.
  • More area for content and other job types has been made available at the bottom of the email.

Wireframe of Contractors Page from Axure.

  • Contractor structure, both in categorisation and detail, reflects that from the email.
  • Search functionality has been added.
  • Header navigation has been revised.

Wireframe of Contractor Detail Page from Axure.

  • Contractor structure is consistent with the email.
  • A form has been provided to contact 10collective, along with email addresses and phone numbers as previously.

Screenshot of new email - top.

  • Color has been added, with all calls to action in pink.
  • Contractor structure has been revised following user testing.
  • Personalisation of email has been added.

Screenshot of new email - bottom.

  • Call to action for permanent hire is different.

Screenshot of new contractors page.

  • Color has been added, with all calls to action in pink.
  • Contractor structure has been revised following user testing.

Screenshot of new contractor detail page.

  • Color has been added, with all calls to action in pink.
  • Contractor structure has been revised following user testing.
